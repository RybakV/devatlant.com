La French Tech

French Tech is a label assigned to French metropolises recognised for their startup ecosystem. It is also a name used by technologically innovative French businesses on all continents. The French Tech aims to provide a strong common visual identity to French startups as well as to promote entrepreneurial exchange between them.

Wikipedia

Activity

Integrate

One of the goals of La French Tech is the integration of different organisations which have technological, educational, financial and entrepreneurial potential to help french startups, investors and talented foreign entrepreneurs

Help

La French Tech works not only with startups but also with existing enterprises. It has two huge programs: Bourse French Tech - help for french startups, Pass French Tech - help for developing enterprises. For these purposes La French Tech has a fund of investments of 200M€

Internalisation

La French Tech tries to expand its activity all over the world and attract foreign investors and entrepreneurs. It has a special program for foreign startups to help them start work in France

French Tech Ticket

The French Tech Ticket is a program for non-french entrepreneurs from all countries who want to realise their ideas and start their business in France

Prize is €12 500 euro for your development

You will be able to communicate with professionals and get precious advices

Help in administrative procedures, living and working difficulties
